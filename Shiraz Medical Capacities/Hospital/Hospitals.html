The ancient city of Shiraz, dating back to the age of fourty-five, has always been known as the medical center of South-Eastern medicine, and has hosted thousands of foreign patients, especially from the Arabian Gulf states annually. With more than dozens of well-equipped and specialized academic, charity and private hospitals, the city offers a wide range of medical services by experienced physicians.
All hospitals in Shiraz (both private and public) are in the care of the quality of providing health care services and observing international standards under the strict supervision of the Shiraz University of Medical Sciences and the Shiraz Medical Medical Organization.


Some of the widespread medical services provided to modern and domestic patients in Shiraz include:


-Organ transplants: such as liver transplantation, kidney, pancreas, bone marrow
-Cerebrovascular angioplasty and MRI tests
- Liver angioplasty
- Chemotherapy and radiotherapy for a variety of cancers
- Different surgeries of the eye using the most advanced facilities and methods of the day
- Variety of beauty treatments and skin and hair care services and the use of the best techniques for weight loss
-Advanced cardiovascular surgery
- Orthopedic services and hip and knee replacement
- Treatment of infertility in men and women in different ways and performing IVF.
